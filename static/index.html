SpeciesLookup is a free species query service from the Museum of Vertebrate Zoology. Click on the map to view a list of amphibian species at that location. The codebase was developed by Chenyu Shi, an undergraduate UC Berkeley

You can call the query service yourself with:
 https://specieslookup.berkeley.edu/search/[longitude,latitude]
 https://specieslookup.berkeley.edu/search_json/[longitude,latitude]
